Tourist's Favourites!

Skate Park

Surabaya's Skate Park is a vibrant space for the city's skateboarding enthusiasts and youth culture. With well-designed ramps, rails, and bowls, the park attracts both beginners and experienced skaters looking to hone their skills. It serves as a social hub where the local skateboarding community gathers, fostering creativity, freedom, and a sense of camaraderie in the heart of Surabaya.

Tunjungan Street

Tunjungan Street is one of Surabaya’s most iconic and bustling avenues, rich in history and culture. This historic street is home to a blend of modern shopping centers and preserved colonial-era buildings, offering visitors a glimpse into the city’s architectural past. Tunjungan Street comes alive at night with vibrant lights, street vendors, and local eateries, making it a lively spot for both locals and tourists to explore.

Local's Favourites!

Orasis Art Space

Orasis Space is a creative hub in Surabaya, designed to inspire and nurture the city’s artistic and entrepreneurial community. It offers a collaborative environment for artists, innovators, and freelancers to connect, exchange ideas, and work on exciting projects. The space frequently hosts workshops, exhibitions, and events that celebrate innovation and creativity, making it a hotspot for the city’s growing creative scene.

Museum Pendidikan

Museum Pendidikan is a dedicated educational park in Surabaya, designed to promote learning and outdoor activities for children and families. It provides a vibrant space where kids can explore nature while engaging in fun, educational experiences, such as interactive displays and environmental learning stations. The park is a perfect blend of recreation and education, fostering curiosity and a love for learning in a refreshing outdoor setting.
